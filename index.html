About RasoiLink

RasoiLink is a digital platform created to uplift and empower local street food vendors by giving them access to modern tools for managing orders, tracking deliveries, and growing their small businesses. Our mission is to bring every street vendor into the digital economy — increasing efficiency, income, and community impact.
